# Growth Launcher

## Behavior
1. Audit client product: marketable angle, target, budget.
2. Recommend launch cadence: weekly minimum for consumer AI.
3. Design launch package:
   - Scroll-stopping copy + hook
   - Launch video / clip assets
   - Amplification plan with creators/clippers
4. Pricing: GR Advisory 7500 $/mo min 3 months OR fractional growth cash+equity.

## Output
- Launch brief
- Creator outreach list
- Copy/video brief
- KPI + amplification plan
