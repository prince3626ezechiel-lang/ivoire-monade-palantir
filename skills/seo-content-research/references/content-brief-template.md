# Content Brief Template

## Overview

Use this template to structure every SEO content brief. The goal is a self-contained brief that a writer (human or AI) can follow without asking questions.

## Template

```markdown
# Content Brief: [Primary Keyword]

## Target Market
**Country:** [Country Full Name]
**Language:** [English / Local Language]

## Primary Keyword
`[exact keyword]`

## Secondary Keywords
- [related keyword 1]
- [related keyword 2]
- [related keyword 3]
- [related keyword 4]
- [related keyword 5]

## Search Intent
[Commercial / Informational / Transactional / Navigational]

## Target Audience
[Who is searching for this keyword? What stage of buying cycle?]

## Title Suggestions
1. [Title option 1 — include primary keyword near start]
2. [Title option 2 — different angle]
3. [Title option 3 — question format or list format]

## Competitor Analysis
_Run serp-analyzer.py for this keyword_

| Rank | URL | Type | Strength | Gap to exploit |
|:----:|-----|:----:|:--------:|:--------------:|
| 1 | example.com | Factory | Strong about page | No buyer guide content |

## Recommended Structure

1. **H2: [Heading 1]** — Why this section
   - Key points to cover
   - Angle to differentiate from competitors
   - Data/statistics to include

2. **H2: [Heading 2]** — Why this section
   - Key points to cover
   - Angle to differentiate

3. **H2: [Heading 3]** — Why this section
   - Key points to cover
   - Angle to differentiate

## Key Data Points to Include
- Market size / growth statistic
- Price comparison
- Specification table
- Process explanation (step-by-step)

## EEAT Signals Needed
- [ ] Factory/production photos
- [ ] Certifications (ISO, SGS, etc.)
- [ ] Client logos or testimonials
- [ ] Case study or project example
- [ ] Author bio with industry experience
- [ ] External references to authoritative sources

## Internal Links
- Link to: [Product Category] — anchor: "[primary keyword]"
- Link to: [About Us] — anchor: "our factory"
- Link to: [Contact] — anchor: "get a quote"

## AI Detection Strategy
- Avoid structured emoji prefixes
- No numbered lists longer than 3 items — convert to prose
- Vary paragraph length
- Add 1-2 personal observations
- Use "we", "our" (first person factory voice)
- Avoid "furthermore", "moreover", "in conclusion"

## Word Count Target
[1500-2500 words]

## Call to Action
[What should the reader do next? — Contact us / Get a quote / Download spec sheet]
```

## Example: Filled Brief

Here's a real example using "heavy duty tarp manufacturer canada":

```markdown
# Content Brief: heavy duty tarp manufacturer canada

## Target Market
**Country:** Canada
**Language:** English

## Primary Keyword
`heavy duty tarp manufacturer canada`

## Search Intent
Commercial — buyer looking for a supplier

## Target Audience
Canadian construction/agriculture procurement managers

## Title Suggestions
1. Heavy Duty Tarp Manufacturer Canada | Direct From Factory
2. Canadian Heavy Duty Tarps — Wholesale Pricing from China Factory
3. How to Choose a Heavy Duty Tarp Manufacturer in Canada [2026 Guide]

## Recommended Structure

1. **Canada's Demand for Heavy Duty Tarps** — Market context
2. **Why Canadian Businesses Import from China** — Cost/quality comparison
3. **Quality Standards: What to Look For** — GSM, UV rating, reinforced hems
4. **Our Factory: Manufacturing Process** — EEAT signal
5. **Shipping from Our Factory to Canada** — Logistics detail (30-45 days sea)
6. **Custom Tarps for Canadian Winter Conditions** — Differentiator
7. **FAQ** — Voice search capture

## EEAT Signals
- Cold-weather testing photos
- Canadian client testimonials
- Shipping documentation

## AI Detection
- Write in "we" voice (factory owner perspective)
- Mention specific Canadian provinces (Alberta oil fields, BC fishing)
```

## Checklist

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Secondary keywords distributed across H2 sections
- [ ] Each H2 addresses a different aspect of buyer's question
- [ ] At least 3 EEAT signals included
- [ ] Internal links planned (at least 3)
- [ ] CTA matches search intent
- [ ] Content passes AI detection check before final
